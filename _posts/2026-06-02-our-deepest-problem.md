# Life's target

• We all set targets/goals/plans for ourself in life. My example: running sub-20.

## Question: Have you ever set yourself a target in life? How did you go at hitting it? (7 min group discussion).

• Story --> My friend the world's best archer.
• Our whole life is like an arrow. We live how we want and draw the target around it.

# What should be our target in life?

• Christians believe every human is created for a purpose: to love God and one another.
• The Bible teaches that every human misses the target. Story --> Genesis 3.
• Sin = missing the target.
• From this, all sorts of problems enter the world.

## Question: What is the world's deepest problem right now? (7 min group discussion)

# Jesus' target

• G.K Chesterton: "I am."
• Personal story. My faith journey. Discovery I lived for self.
• Problem because we cut ourselves off from God.
• Jesus' addressed lots of problems in his day: sickness, power abuse, natural disaster, social alienation.
• Jesus' target was to address the root problem of humanity: sin. 1 Tim 1:15.
