## What Sabbath is
• SBT = "stop" or "rest". Holy time for God. Incl. days, festivals and years (7th, 49th).
• "The SBT" = a day (Fri–Sat) of rest ("stop") from typical work, set apart for God.
• SBT != physical necessity (like sleep). We can live without SBT; it's called slavery.
• Can't stop work = slavery (to exploitative employers, success, parental expectations). 
• SBT != salvation necessity; don't need SBT to be rescued, but sign that rescued.
• SBT = physical and spiritual gift from God that we might enjoy life with God better.
• A delight: SBT helped Israel love others properly (refreshment for all; read 23:10-12).
--- Ex 23:10-12 “For six years you are to sow your fields and harvest the crops,
but during the seventh year let the land lie unplowed and unused.
Then the poor among your people may get food from it,
and the wild animals may eat what is left.
Do the same with your vineyard and your olive grove.
Six days do your work, but on the seventh day do not work, so that your ox
and your donkey may rest, and so that the slave born in your household
and the foreigner living among you may be refreshed." ---
• Social aspects: gathered worship, singing, hospitality, big meals, friends/fam/spouse.
• Mission aspects: weird act; testimony to the watching world.
• Important for Israel: God commanded it, disobedience carried penalties (read 31:14).
--- Ex 31:14 "Observe the Sabbath, because it is holy to you.
Anyone who desecrates it is to be put to death;
those who do any work on that day must be cut off from their people." ---
• Boundaries: prepare house, food, etc (took work) + no work talk.
• A delight: SBT helped Israel love God properly as sign of covenant (read 31:17).
--- Ex 31:17 "It will be a sign between me and the Israelites forever,
for in six days the Lord made the heavens and the earth,
and on the seventh day he rested and was refreshed.’" ---
• Patterned after God's creation of world (Gen 1:31 = "very good", God rested).
• Religious aspects: worship, scripture/prayer/meditation on creation, rescue, future.
• Not a burden but a blessing; not a master but a servant; Jesus' point (read Mk 2:27).
--- (Mark 2:27) “The Sabbath was made for man, not man for the Sabbath." ---
• Recap: a gift from God, holy time for God, for love (others/God) & witness to world.
• Something for us today? My answer yes.

## How to rediscover Sabbath today (3 Steps)
• 1. Embrace the wisdom of the Sabbath.
• Is Sabbath bad? Jesus had issue with Pharisees; how they did it, not that they did it.
• Pharisees distorted SBT—human rules layered above (e.g. didn't do good; Matt 12:12).
• The Pharisees got SBT wrong; but the SBT is not itself wrong.
• We're not under law but we uphold it; 10C = good. SBT is rooted in creation.
• Jesus taught the commands (SoM) and refocused on the heart: "But I tell you..."
• Practiced throughout church history (Sunday). Ford 1926 2-day (Jews/Christians).
• Spiritual and bio gift: Not physical death, but a slow spiritual decay, if we neglect SBT.
• 2. Acknowledge keeping SBT = spiritual battle. 2 sides = work and distractions.
• Spiritual battle #1/1: we idolise work.
• Rest is seen as weak; value comes from productivity
• Why we strive for $ = not to get valuable things, but to signal we are something valuable
• Repent! Our value != what we make... BUT = that we are made by God...
• Industry confusion re rest: combine work & rest (e.g. Rombii pool, Google pong).
• Purpose of SBT mainly != we can work better... BUT = we can worship better.
• Spiritual Battle #2/2: we love our distractions.
• National battle: AU used to stop on Sunday. 50s laws close shops. Sundays for God.
• Tom Frame: golden era church 1945–56. '45 end of WWII. '56? TV in Aus.
--- AB Howard Mowll: "Perhaps the greatest opportunity of all time
to reach the non-churchgoer." ---
• Losing battle: Media companies wanted every square inch of our calendar.
• Church battle: Sunday worship eroded. Parents went to Evensong (pre Countdown).
• Battle w selfishness: if I get a day off, who's it for? Even w many days off.
• Battle w godlessness: 1:1 time with God. Is God is a group-only friend?
• 3. Rejoice in Jesus died for our sins, took the penalty, and won us an eternal Sabbath.
• There is no condemnation for thos in Christ! No judgement re SBT (read Col 2:16).
--- (Col 2:16) do not let anyone judge you by what you eat or drink,
or with regard to a religious festival,
a New Moon celebration or a Sabbath day. ---
• 4. Commit to keeping a Sabbath day.
• Scary part! My journey w Soph... realisation of sin, need to repent. Thurs SBT.
• Last week - common question: what will I do on the day? Coming to that.
• Choose 24-hour period; doesn't matter which day (see Rom 14:5).
--- Rom 4:5 "One person considers one day more sacred than another;
another considers every day alike. Each of them should be fully
convinced in their own mind." ---
• Should it be Sunday? Yes if that's most helpful. No if it's not.
--- ( A.J. Swaboda: Subversive Sabbath) "The church is a place to learn how to rest.
It is not the place we go to burn out." ---
• Loosely map out day with good things. Sunday e.g. Meals/Prayer/Study/Recreation
• Don't be task, but time-oriented on SBT. Entropy = curse (e.g. email) or blessing.
• Embrace the social aspects (see above; align sabbath w others?).
• Embrace the religious aspects (see above; reminder of eternal rest, Heb 4:1-10).
• Remember: One' work is another’s rest (e.g. baking/painting/writing).
• Set boundaries (prep; talk w boss/fam; no work talk; less distractions / no media).
• Potential result: others see the weird sign, become intrigued, know God / eternal rest.
• Enjoy the gift God gives us!
