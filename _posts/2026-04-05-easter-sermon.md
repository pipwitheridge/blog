# Notes

Alex Honnold analogy: something in his brain preventing him from the fear of death, something in the amygdola. That is why he comes off as so courages. Peter and the apostles had something in their brain that prevented them from the fear of death—and that is that they had seen the risen Jesus. Therefore they were not afraid. The resurrection changes the way you view life.
