# Notes

Notice how quickly Israel moves to make an idol at the slightest hint of God not being there.

Notice how culturally-conditioned their idol is. They turn to what the cultures around them turn to.

Notice how the things they make an idol of (gold, a cow) are not in themselves bad, but become bad when they are made ultimate.

Notice that it happens as a group, and the mob-mentality makes it acceptable for the individual idolator. Communal idolatry makes individual idolatry seem alright.
