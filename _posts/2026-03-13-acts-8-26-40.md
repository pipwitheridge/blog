This post was written as a talk for youth group looking at Acts 8:26–40.

---

Do you ever wonder whether God cares about you?

How does the kingdom of God spread? Questions, conversations.

Your view of God shouldn't be determined by the welcome you receive in any religious setting.

My grandparents were missionaries to Ethiopia.

Evangelism is dangerous but necessary.

Asking questions is dangerous but necessary.

The importance of baptism.

The humiliation of Jesus. He endured injustice so that we could get grace.

How God loves the outsider (ethnic/sexual minority).
